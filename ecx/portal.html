THE PHILOSOPHY OF CITADEL UNIVERSITY

The University is a center of learning and research of distinction, combining academic excellence with love for humanity, borne out of a God-fearing attitude in accordance with the Baptist traditional of ethical behaviour, social responsibility and democratic ethos.An atmosphere where leaders are raised and valued for transformative change in the society.

CORE VALUES

Integrity

Diligence

Empathy

Responsibility

Accountability


VISION

To raise a people of integrity, purpose, excellence and power who will continually be a change agent in their community and their nation

MISSION

To establish a community of leaders that will carry forward the purpose of why they are created on earth.
